Project Robert – RΩ

Exploration of ethical architectures for autonomous systems

About

RΩ (R-Omega) is an experimental ethical framework for autonomous agents. It focuses on preserving possibility spaces, preventing destructive drift, and keeping systems aligned with human survival and dignity.

Core Principles

  • R1 — Potentiality: Prefer states that expand or preserve the possibility space.
  • R2 — Reciprocity: No intervention should harm others more than the actor accepts for themselves.
  • Safeguards: Avoid collapse, avoid structural destruction, stay within tolerances.
